How to Use

Welcome to Fish Fixer! Follow these steps to diagnose your fish's condition:

  1. Click on the "Diagnose Your Fish" button above.
  2. Fill out the form with details about your fish, symptoms, and environment.
  3. The more you fill out, the better the diagnose will be!
  4. Try and be as concise as possible.
  5. Upload a clear photo of your fish if available.
  6. Submit the form for diagnosis.
  7. Review the diagnosis and recommendations provided.
